What are Soft Skills?

Soft skills are personal abilities that help people communicate, work together and solve problems. During our internship, we improved these skills through teamwork and practical activities.

Teamwork

Working together, sharing ideas and achieving goals as a team.

Communication

Sharing ideas clearly, listening to others and understanding different opinions.

Problem Solving

Finding solutions and dealing with challenges in different situations.

Time Management

Planning tasks and using time effectively.

Responsibility

Being reliable and completing tasks successfully.

Creativity

Developing new ideas and finding different solutions.

Case Study

During our internship, we worked on a case study in groups. We shared ideas, divided tasks and presented our final solution. This improved our teamwork, communication and problem-solving skills.
